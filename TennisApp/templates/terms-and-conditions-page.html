1. About This App

This app is designed exclusively for the Portico Apartments at Toongabbie community to fairly manage access to the shared tennis court. It is a non-commercial platform available only to apartment owners and tenants.

2. Registration and User Information

To ensure fair use, all users must register with:

  • Real name
  • Apartment number
  • Phone number
  • Email address

This information is solely for user identification and will not be shared with third parties.

3. Booking Rules

  • Bookings are on a first-come, first-served basis.
  • Users must cancel bookings they cannot attend.
  • Abuse of the system (e.g., fake bookings, monopolizing the court) may result in account suspension.
